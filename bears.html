About the Chicago Bears

The Chicago Bears are a professional football team based in Chicago, Illinois.

As of November 5th, the Bears are 4-4 on the season and have not been playing well the past 2 weeks. It seems as though they lost all of their momentum before the bye week. The head coach has been under fire for how this team is playing and if this keeps up then the GM Ryan Poles will have no choice but to fire him soon or once the season ends.
